Magic

Advanced Software Design

Description

2018 Spring

Software Development (Team of 3)

Programmer/UX Design

Process Analysis

Magic is my team's winning entry into SIAT's 10th Year Anniversary competition, a conceptual interaction for a wearable bracelet. Tasked with the words "Social Innovation", I worked with Johnson Zhu and Carlo Lai to come up with the idea of enhancing greetings with technology in the background.

The brief wanted us to address "social innovation" in a way that benefitted society. Our team consisted of a 3D modeler, a developer, and myself as designer. Immediately our group constrained the two week competition using our own interests and skillsets to make the project manageable. So we looked to technology trends for inspiration of how to start this idea.

In the timeframe of the project (2013) Google Glasses were discussed extensively in media. For our group the aspect that stood out was the discussion of the benefits of technology without the disruption of technology. As we brainstormed different ideas, it needed to pass that criteria to be considered. Our group wanted to ground our decisions around keeping the human touch in our design concept.
